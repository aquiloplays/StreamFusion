# StreamFusion 1.20.0: reminders

Never forget to stretch, hydrate, or plug your socials again. StreamFusion can now pop **reminders** into your events panel on a schedule — with their own sound.

## ⏰ Reminders
- **Run-time triggers.** Fire a reminder **every N minutes** you're live, or **once at N minutes** into the stream — perfect for "stretch break", "hydrate", or "raid soon". Run-time reminders only fire while you're actually live.
- **Clock triggers.** Or fire at a set **time of day** (e.g. 9:00 PM) — once per day.
- **Right where you'd expect.** Reminders pop up in the **events panel** just like any alert, with their **own reminder chime** so you'll hear them even when you're not looking.
- Set them up in **Settings → Events → Reminders** — add a message, pick the trigger, done. Toggle, preview, or remove any time.
