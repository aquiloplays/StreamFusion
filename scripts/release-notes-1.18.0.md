# StreamFusion 1.18.0: eyes on the whole stream

This one is about never losing the thread mid-stream: a live ticker above your
chat, join alerts from every platform, a viewer list you can actually read, and
your TikTok and Kick support finally counting toward the same hype train.

## ⏱️ New ticker bar

A slim strip above the chat and events feed that keeps the things you keep
glancing for right in front of you:

- **Ad countdown**: a live "Next ad in 2:14" timer that flips to "Ads end in
  0:48" the moment a break starts, so you always know when to wrap a bit or
  hold a story. It reads straight from Twitch and stays accurate even with the
  Stream panel closed.
- **Pinned event**: your last notable event — that big gift, that raid — stays
  pinned so it doesn't scroll away.
- **Viewer count**: one click opens your full viewer list.

Toggle it in Settings → Event Filters. On by default.

## 👥 "User Joined" from every platform, plus a viewer list

- **Cross-platform joins**: TikTok already announced joins (via TikFinity) —
  now Twitch, Kick, and YouTube do too. Those platforms don't expose a real
  join event, so a viewer's first chat message this session counts as their
  join. Opt in per platform in Settings → Event Filters (off by default, since
  it can get chatty on a busy stream).
- **Current viewers list**: click the viewer count in the ticker for a
  searchable list of everyone active in chat this session, across every
  connected platform, each tagged by platform. On Twitch it also folds in your
  live chatter roster — lurkers included — when your connection allows it.

## 🚂 Cross-platform hype train

Your **TikTok gifts** and **Kick subs** now feed the cross-platform hype train
right alongside Twitch. Every contribution is attributed back to the right
supporter, so support from every platform counts toward the same train.

## 📺 Two new OBS overlays

Both are fed straight from StreamFusion — add them as a browser source and
they fill in live:

- **Live viewer count**: a single combined viewer count across your connected
  platforms.
- **Combined goals**: follow and sub goals from every platform in one overlay.
