# StreamFusion 1.16.0: everything is free, for everyone

All gating is gone. Every StreamFusion feature is now available to every
streamer with no supporter account, no sign-in, and no unlock step.

## 🔓 Ungated

- **Stream Info editor**: the "Link your Patreon to unlock" wall is removed.
  Open it from the toolbar and it just works.
- **OBS overlays**: the overlay server no longer serves a gated page; every
  browser-source overlay works for everyone out of the box.
- **Discord+** (event posts, records, bot gateway) and **Theme
  customization**: no entitlement checks anywhere, and the bot gateway is
  never auto-disconnected for entitlement reasons.
- The Early Access settings tab, Patreon and Discord supporter sign-ins,
  supporter badges, and the red supporter dots in settings navigation are all
  removed. The Patreon and Discord entitlement services no longer even ship
  in the app.

If you enjoy StreamFusion and want to support development, a Twitch sub is
always appreciated, and never required.
