# StreamFusion 1.17.0: the settings glow-up

Settings got a full redesign. Same options, same muscle memory (nothing moved
between tabs), dramatically nicer to read and use.

## ✨ New settings experience

- **Sidebar navigation**: the wrapping rows of tabs are gone. Settings now
  has a grouped left sidebar (Setup / Manage / Chat & Alerts / App) with a
  clear highlight on the active page, so 24 sections finally feel organized.
- **A bigger canvas**: the window grows from 560px to 900px wide and uses
  nearly the full height, so you scroll less and see more.
- **Cards instead of walls of text**: every section sits in its own card
  with a real heading, making long pages easy to scan.
- **Comfortable controls**: taller rows with hover highlighting, larger
  checkboxes and toggles in the accent color, better focus states, and
  larger, easier-to-read help text throughout.

Everything is exactly where it was; it just looks and feels the way a
streaming hub should.
