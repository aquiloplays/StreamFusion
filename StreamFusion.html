How to install
  1. Click the button above and download StreamFusion-Setup-x.x.x.exe from the latest release.
  2. Run the installer and follow the prompts.
  3. Launch StreamFusion from your desktop and connect your platforms.
Heads up: Windows may show an "unknown publisher" warning on first install. Click More info → Run anyway. This is normal for independently distributed apps.
